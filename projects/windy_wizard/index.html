Windy Wizard

made by Jaydon Grunsted, Brandon Monge, Christopher Xiang, and Miles Ortolan

including music by Cammie Golba and trailer voice over by C.D.

    a 2D fantasy castle crawling windy platformer with a physics based twist

    In Windy Wizard your family has protected this castle for centuries, as the newest up and coming defender within your family you have a lot on your plate since the castle has been completely overrun by monsters. In this 2D action platformer, you’ll master your wind magic abilities as you navigate the treacherous terrain and outsmart your oponents. It’s all about strategy and creativity—using the environment to your advantage to defeat your adversaries. Are you ready to unleash the wind and rise to the challenge?

    Link to Itch.io page Link to GameJolt page

    made in unity and scripted in C#.

    I worked on character movement, controls, and the physics and behaviors to do with the air jump/shot mechanic

    I also made the red goblin enemy that appears, worked on the ui elements including cool particle effects and helped with level design.
